Lalo

Description

Lalo is a vegatable found in Haiti, as you can observe it the food in this picture is not Lalo. As I didn't have images of lalo nearby I use this picture with that name for the test.

Please do not take this dish for Lalo. it's not lalo.

Ingredients

Below you will find the most important ingredients for lalo dish

  • Meat(beef,goat,chicken,tucky,etc).
  • Litle crabs
  • Lalo vegatable.
  • pepper
  • spices

Steps to prepare lalo

Below you will find some steps in the making of lalo

  1. Spice the meat.
  2. Boil the meat.
  3. Clean Lalo vegetable
  4. Boil lalo vegetable for 30mns
  5. Boil the crabs for 10mns
  6. Brew the meat and the crabs with the vegatable while boiling.
