Ragout

Description

In the countryside of Haiti, they prepare many special dishes,ragout is one of them. The people like it very much and every weekend each people wish to have a bowl of ragout.

Ingredients

Below you will find the most important ingredients for ragout dish

  • Meat(head, guts, paws, feet) of beef or goat
  • vegetables
  • potatoes, carrots,malanga, suit potatoes, plantains, yams
  • pepper
  • spices

Steps to prepare a ragout

Below you will find some steps in the making of ragout

  1. Spice the meat.
  2. Clean the vegetables.
  3. peel all the ingredients to people
  4. Boil the meat for two hours and the add the potatoes, carrots etc.
  5. add the vegetables on the top.
  6. let it boil for 30mns
